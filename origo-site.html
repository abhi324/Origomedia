Questions Answered

Frequently
Asked.

For Brands

Brand FAQs.

What kinds of brands does Origo work with? +
We exclusively work with beauty and skincare brands — including skincare, dermacosmetics, makeup, and perfumes. This focus means our creator network is perfectly aligned with your target audience.
How long does a campaign take to deliver? +
Our standard campaigns deliver in 7–12 days from kickoff. This includes creator briefing, content creation, review, and live posting. Larger campaigns may take slightly longer.
What is the delivery guarantee? +
Every campaign comes with a full delivery guarantee. If all agreed content isn't delivered within the campaign timeline, we bring in additional creators at no extra cost to you — until the full deliverable is complete.
Do I get to approve content before it goes live? +
Yes. We include a content review stage in every campaign. You'll see creator content before it's posted and can request reasonable revisions aligned with the original brief.
What is the Founding Partner Program? +
The Founding Partner Program offers the first two brands introductory campaign rates, direct access to the founding team, early input into how we build our processes, and priority creator matching. Only 2 slots available.
For Creators

Creator FAQs.

Who can join the Origo creator network? +
We work with nano and micro creators (1k–100k followers) who create content in the beauty and skincare space. Engagement quality matters more than follower count.
How does payment work? +
Rates are agreed upfront before any content is created. Payment is processed after content goes live and is confirmed. We have a clear, no-surprise payment process for every creator.
What type of content will I be asked to make? +
Primarily Instagram Reels and Stories — authentic, creator-led content that showcases beauty and skincare products in a natural, engaging way. You'll always receive a detailed brief before starting.
How quickly will I hear back after applying? +
We review all applications within 48 hours. If your profile is a fit for our current brand briefs, we'll reach out via DM or email to confirm your spot in the network.
